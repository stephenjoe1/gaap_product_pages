Benefits of GOV.UK Pay

GOV.UK Pay makes it easier for the public and businesses to pay for government services. It also makes it much easier for service teams to process these payments.

Benefits to service teams include being able to:

  • troubleshoot payment queries easily
  • issue refunds and chargebacks
  • reconcile payments
  • generate financial reports for specific time periods
  • send receipt emails to users
  • invite team members and issue specific permission levels
  • select different payment methods and card types

Benefits to those paying government include:

  • a better and more consistent payment experience across government services
  • a choice regarding how to pay
  • more accessible payment pages

How GOV.UK Pay works

GOV.UK Pay builds technical integrations with payment service providers to make it simple for services across central government to take online payments. It will also reduce processing costs for government.

  • give government teams more options to take online payments, including credit and debit cards
  • increase efficiency in government payment processing by reducing the need for manual payment processing
  • make it easier for teams to reconcile payments and issue refunds
